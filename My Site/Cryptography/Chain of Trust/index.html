Terms:

- Certificate Authority (CA): Trusted third-party organization or entity that issues digital certificates to verify the identity of entities, such as websites, individuals, or organizations

- Root Certificate Authority (RCA): Highest level of trust and authority in the certificate hierarchy, issues digital certificates, either directly to entities or to intermediate Certificate Authorities

- Intermediate Certificate Authority (ICA): Certificate Authority that operates under the trust and authority of a Root Certificate Authority (Root CA)

- Root Store / Trust Store: Collection of trusted Root Certificats what the client uses in order to verify the server's trust
